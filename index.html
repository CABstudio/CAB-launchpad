Pre-Launch Flight Check

🟡 Connecting to Firebase Database...
Launch rule: Launch only if every Hard Blocker passes. For the Important section, allow at most 1 fail, and only if it has a clear post-launch fix that does not affect spending, saving, or first-session clarity.

Hard Blockers

1) New group-owned universe is fully wired
Pass condition: every monetization/system reference points to the new universe, and nothing still depends on the old personal experience.
2) Monetization works end-to-end
Test every live launch product: Founder’s Pack, VIP, Collect All, Server Luck, Admin Weather, Currency packs, Claw purchases, Any quick-buy pad offer.
Pass condition: zero misgrants, zero duplicate grants, zero “paid but didn’t get it” cases.
3) First 10 minutes are clear
Use tomorrow’s final playtest for this.
Pass condition: players always know what the next step is, and the opening loop feels sticky, not confusing.
4) Pacing is validated after the rebalance
Run at least: average luck path, lucky path, very lucky path.
Pass condition: lucky players feel rewarded, but no path trivializes the game or stalls into misery.

Important

5) Claw locks and gates are readable
Pass condition: no confusion, no “why can’t I buy this?” moments.
6) Minimum analytics are in place
You do not need big-studio analytics, but you need enough to not guess blindly.
Pass condition: after launch, you will be able to answer what players are doing instead of relying on vibes.
7) Monetization/world presentation is not too dense
Pass condition: the game feels like a game first, shop second.
8) Post-launch plan exists before launch
Pass condition: launch is the start of a plan, not the end of one.
9) Launch stays policy-simple
Since you are not adding lootboxes/wheels yet:
Pass condition: launch monetization is simple, readable, and low-risk.
10) You know the top 3 launch risks
Before launch, force yourselves to write these down:
Pass condition: you can name the 3 biggest threats without hesitating.

Final launch decision

🟢 Green light

Launch if:

  • all Hard Blockers pass
  • Important section has 0–1 fails
  • no fail involves saving, purchases, permissions, or first-session clarity

🟡 Yellow light

Delay 1–3 days if:

  • any monetization product misgrants
  • the new group-owned universe still has mismatched IDs
  • players are confused in the first 10 minutes
  • the economy still clearly snowballs or clearly stalls

🔴 Red light

Do not launch if:

  • any paid item is unreliable
  • the save system is unstable
  • the wrong experience IDs/products are wired
  • first-time players do not understand the loop
